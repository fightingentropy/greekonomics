Loving Yourself Is A Choice

Just like loving others

RUBY DEC 29, 2024

I always hear people say that love is a choice. But they only ever say it in the context of intimate relationships.

I've come to the realization that loving yourself is also a choice.

In my late teens and very early 20s, I don't think I loved myself very much. I walked through life a zombie. There was a cloud of thick, heavy fog around my choices. I made decisions hastily, and these decisions held the power to destroy me. And I didn't seem to care.

I think that's the worst part.

In intimate relationships, you have to wake up every day and make the choice to love the other person. You have to forget yesterday and begin the relationship anew. You have to decide that you want your life above any other. It's intentional.

I wasn't intentional with my own life. And I don't think many people are. It might surprise them when I say it's because they don't love themselves—but it's true. Love is intention.
